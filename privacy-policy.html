Log Files

As like most websites, we also gather certain information automatically and store it in our log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data. This information is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

We use this information, which does not identify any individual users, to analyze trends, to administer the site, to track user movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base.

IP addresses are tied to personally identifiable information to help us customize your future visits to our website. However, we would like to reiterate that this information is not shared with any third parties for their promotional purposes, except as provided under Disclosure of Information clause below.

We store cookies on your computer when you visit our website. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes and the primary purpose of these cookies are to analyze how our users move within the website. Our cookies let you view customized pages while transacting or interacting with us. Our cookies do not have confidential or personally identifiable information. Since we use session ID cookies, they do not track a user after leaving our website. A session ID cookie expires when you close the browser. It is required if you want to buy an insurance policy online through us. We also use analytic tools to track visitor traffic on our website.

Some of our third-party service providers such as chat support use cookies on our site. We have no access to or control over these cookies. This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by www.bimafy.com only and does not cover the use of cookies by any third parties.
